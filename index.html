What is real

The four input formats (text, PDF, image, voice).

The extraction pattern — unstructured input to structured fields with confidence indicators.

The review-before-commit workflow.

The "needs your attention" flags for ambiguity.

What is mocked

The extraction itself is pattern-matched to four sample inputs.

No actual AI is running in this prototype.

No connection to EventsAir, Cvent, or any other platform.

The "Approve & Push" button does nothing yet.

What comes next

Real Claude-powered extraction on any input format.

Direct delegate-record linking in Cvent via API.

Side-by-side source highlighting on every extracted field.

Full audit log of what was extracted, approved, and pushed.
